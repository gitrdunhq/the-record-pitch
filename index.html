The Problem
Institutional Amnesia

Every meeting produces decisions. Most of them disappear. The rename vs. delete confusion. "I thought we agreed to X." "Who approved this?" "I wasn't in that meeting." These aren't process failures or people failures. They're memory failures — the organization forgot something it knew.

The tools that exist today don't fix this. Otter, Fireflies, Notion AI, Microsoft Copilot — all do the same thing: transcribe, chunk, send to GPT-4, return bullet points. That's not intelligence. That's grep with a nicer UI.

They produce transcripts. We're building a decision engine.
